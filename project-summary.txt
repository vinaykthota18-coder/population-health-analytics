Population Health Analytics Project Summary

Goal:
Identify high-risk chronic disease patients using longitudinal healthcare data.

Focus Areas:
- Risk stratification
- Chronic disease trend analysis
- Preventive care planning
- Population health insight generation

Methods:
- Data cleaning
- Cohort-level analysis
- Trend interpretation
- Risk-based grouping

Outcome:
This project supported healthcare decision-making by highlighting high-risk populations and helping prioritize preventive interventions.
